Information on CyberSecurity and cyberattacks

"CyberSecurity" is the practice of protecting networks, systems and programs from digital attacks from nefarious sources.

Cyber Attacks are often aimed at Companies or individuals. These attacks can include:

Acessing, Altering, or Destroying any sensiitive information relating to the person or company

Interupting Normal buisness practices

Wiring money or extorting users/companies via "RansomWare" such as CryptoLocker

Hacking accses into websites and then selling that accses to the highest bidder



CyberSecurity is major threat to bigger companies, on average a cybersecurity attack happens every 39 seconds.

Aswell as this, only about 5% of all company folders in the world are properly protected from cyber attacks

In the first half of 2020 alone, 36 BILLION records were breached, 86% of those being financially motivated, and another 10% being espionage attempts.



Following Covid 19, the frequency and proportion of cyberattacks increased by a staggering 35%, as many companies moved and still stay in work from home conditions.

Aswell, with the rise of AI technologies in recent years, its become much easier to rapidly search a companies database or files for what you are looking for.



There are lots of ways for those with malicious intent can gain acsess to your databases/files

the most common way is to send fake messages, usually emails or texts, with a link to a webpage or program that then invades your computer

This type of scam is comently known as "phishing"

in the year of 2021 alone, nearly 1 billion emails were affected by Phising attempts.
